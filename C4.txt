INSTRUCTIONS FOR CONNECT 4:-

Connect 4 is a game played vertically. The aim, similar to that of TicTacToe, is to 
get any 4 of your coins consecutively in the same row, column, or diagonal. 
On selecting to column number, your coin will fall on top of whichever coin is already in that column.
Game ends when 4 of any coin are in a line in whichever direction.

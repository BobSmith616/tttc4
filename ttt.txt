INSTRUCTIONS FOR TICTACTOE:
Players play their turn alternately.The aim of the game is to fill any full row, column, 
or diagonal with your shape (X or O).
To play your turn, enter the row number and then the column number corresponding to the 
slot you want to play your move in.For example:- [1,3] for top top right corner.
Enjoy!
